About Us

Moulescoomb Boxing Academy is a youth-focused, community-rooted boxing programme dedicated to developing confident, disciplined, and skilled young athletes across Sussex.

Founded by passionate coaches, our academy provides structured training for children aged 10–15. We believe in using boxing as a vehicle to teach life skills and help young people grow both physically and mentally.

Why We Developed the Program

Too often, children can get lost in traditional boxing gyms where training lacks clear structure, progression, and short-term goals. Without these elements, it’s easy for young people to become demotivated or overlooked.

We developed this structured 7-level programme to ensure every child receives:

  • ✅ Purposeful, goal-oriented coaching
  • ✅ A clear path for progress and recognition
  • ✅ Personal development support
  • ✅ The confidence and resilience to thrive both in and out of the ring

Our focus is on building skill, discipline, and self-belief — not just producing boxers, but empowering young people for life.

Who Are the Coaches?

Your children will be coached by our highly experienced team:

Nick Bell – Head Coach

Nick is a multiple-time national boxing champion with 68 competitive bouts under England Boxing. He began boxing at the age of 11 and has achieved the following:

  • Schools Championship Finalist
  • Junior ABA Champion
  • Junior NABGC Finalist
  • Senior NABGC Champion
  • Senior Elite Championship Finalist

With over 20 years of coaching experience, Nick brings a wealth of technical knowledge and a deep commitment to helping young people develop discipline, resilience, and confidence.

Hayley Weller – Head Coach

Hayley began training and competing in kickboxing and Wing Chun at the age of 12, before becoming a highly accomplished competitive boxer in 2008 with 25 bouts under England Boxing. Her accolades include:

  • 3x Southern Counties Champion
  • 2x National Development Finalist
  • Senior Elite National Finalist
  • Haringey Box Cup Finalist

With over 12 years of coaching experience, Hayley is passionate about supporting young people through structured boxing development.

Together, Nick and Hayley lead the academy with a shared vision: to build champions inside and outside the ring.
