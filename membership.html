Become a Member

Moulescoomb Boxing Academy offers a clear, progressive programme designed to help young people build confidence, discipline, fitness, and boxing skills in a safe and supportive environment.

Programme Overview

  • Ages: 10–15
  • 7 progressive levels (12-week courses + 1-day assessments)
  • Pathways to competition or coaching leadership

Membership Fees

Fee Type Amount Frequency
Enrolment Fee £20 Per level
Monthly Tuition £45 Monthly
Grading Fee £30 Every 3 months

What’s Included

  • Structured weekly training sessions
  • Assessment and certification every 3 months
  • Progress tracking through 7-level system
  • Opportunities for competition or leadership roles
  • Access to branded kit and equipment

How to Join

  1. Click the Join button below to register via Gymcatch
  2. Pay your enrolment and first month’s fee
  3. Attend orientation and begin your child’s journey
