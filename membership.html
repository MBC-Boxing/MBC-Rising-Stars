Membership Fees

At Moulescoomb Boxing Academy, we offer a structured pathway for young boxers to develop their skills and confidence. Membership includes access to our progressive 7-level program, led by certified England Boxing coaches, in a safe and inclusive environment.

  • Monthly Membership: £45
  • Enrolment Fee: £20 per level
  • Assessment/Grading Fee: £30 every 3 months
  • Additional costs for kit (see Welcome Pack)

We believe in fostering personal development, fitness, and resilience in all our members. Join us and watch your child grow both in and out of the ring!
