Membership Fees

Monthly Membership Fee: £45 per month

This includes weekly structured sessions led by qualified coaches and access to our full skills development programme. Payments are collected monthly via Glofox.

One Time Enrolment Fee: £20

This one-time fee covers each child's development pack, admin costs, and record tracking for that level.

Assessment Fee: £39 every 3 months

This covers progress evaluations, certificates, and coach feedback. Assessments take place at the end of each level block.

Additional costs may apply for official kit, gloves, or events. See the Welcome Pack for full details.
