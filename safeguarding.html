Safeguarding at Moulescoomb Boxing Academy

We are committed to providing a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment for all children and young people. Our safeguarding practices are fully aligned with England Boxing's national safeguarding framework.

  • All coaches and volunteers are DBS-checked and trained in safeguarding protocols
  • A qualified safeguarding officer is present during all sessions
  • Clear, confidential reporting pathways for concerns or incidents
  • Annual safeguarding and child protection training for all staff
  • Safe recruitment policies for all roles involving contact with children

If you have any concerns, please contact our Designated Safeguarding Lead (DSL) directly. Their details are displayed at our venue and available upon request.
