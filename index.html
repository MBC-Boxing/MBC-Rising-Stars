About Us

Welcome to Moulescoomb Boxing Kids Club — where young athletes learn discipline, respect, and determination through the art of boxing. Led by Head Coach Nick Bell and Deputy Head Coach Hayley Weller, we aim to build champions in and out of the ring.

Programmes

Our Academy helps children progress through structured boxing levels. Every 3 months, athletes are assessed and graded based on skill, commitment, and development.

  • Enrolment Fee: £20 per level (every 3 months)
  • Grading/Assessment Fee: £30 per quarter
  • Pathway to become a carded boxer

How to Join

Joining is simple! Enrol online or visit us at Moulsecoomb Amateur Boxing Club. Your child can begin their boxing journey with a trial session, then enrol fully into the Rising Stars Academy.
