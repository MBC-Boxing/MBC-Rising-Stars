Why Parents Choose Moulescoomb Boxing Academy

  • Safe, inclusive, and structured environment for all children
  • Focus on building fitness, emotional resilience, and confidence
  • Emphasis on discipline, respect, and teamwork values

Our 7-Level Pathway

Each child progresses through our carefully structured curriculum, starting with foundational skills and building towards competitive boxing or leadership roles. Each level emphasizes personal growth, discipline, and achievement — fostering success inside and outside the ring.

Your Child's Safety Comes First

All coaches at Moulescoomb Boxing Academy are DBS-checked, safeguarding-trained, and committed to creating a safe, supportive environment. We prioritize the wellbeing of every participant through rigorous safeguarding policies and best practices.
