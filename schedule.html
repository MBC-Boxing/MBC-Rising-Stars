Weekly Training Schedule

Our training schedule is designed around our structured 7-level progression programme. Sessions are grouped by age and ability to ensure children train with peers at a similar stage. Each level runs for 13 weeks (12 training sessions + 1 assessment day).

What Happens in a Typical Session?

  • 10 min warm-up & mobility
  • 15 min skill development
  • 20 min drills and technique practice
  • 10 min fitness, agility, or resilience training
  • 5 min cooldown & feedback

Bookings

All sessions must be booked through our Gymcatch page. Spaces are limited and secured on a first-come, first-served basis.
