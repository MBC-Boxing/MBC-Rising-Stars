What Happens in a Typical Session?

  • Warm-up: Cardio, mobility, dynamic stretches
  • Technical Skills: Footwork, stance, guard, punches, defense
  • Conditioning: Circuits to boost fitness and agility
  • Partner Drills: Safe, non-contact technical work
  • Cool Down: Stretches, reflection, and goal setting
