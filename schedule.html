What Happens in a Typical Session?

Each training session at Moulescoomb Boxing Academy is structured to balance fitness, skill-building, and confidence development:

  • Warm-up: Light cardio, mobility drills, and dynamic stretches.
  • Skill Development: Footwork drills, stance, guard, punches, and defensive techniques.
  • Conditioning: Fun fitness circuits and activities to improve strength and stamina.
  • Controlled Partner Work: Non-contact technical drills with a partner.
  • Cool-down: Stretching and mental focus exercises to finish the session.

Note: We introduce contact sparring progressively once fundamental skills are established and the child is ready.
